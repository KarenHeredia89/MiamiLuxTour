About Us

Miami Lux Tour is South Florida's premier personal concierge company. Our personalized and custom services create an exclusive Miami experience, leaving you with nothing to do but enjoy.

Let us plan your trip with a house or apartment rental, restaurant reservations, chauffeur, personal shopper, a cruise on a beautiful yacht, fishing charters, and more. Our mission is to do whatever it takes to make your vacation stress and worry free!

Even if insider pointers on where to go and what to do in Miami is all you need, we're here for you!

For the best Miami tips, follow us on Instagram @MiamiLuxTour and please spread the word!

Our trilingual realtor at Elite Sales Group Real Estate can help you find the best condo or house. Let us know if you are interested in buying, selling, or renting property in Miami.
